We would like to remind you of the growing risks associated with spear phishing attacks. Unlike generic phishing attempts, spear phishing messages are carefully crafted and often appear to come from trusted individuals or organizations. Because they feel personal and convincing, they can be especially dangerous.

Please keep the following points in mind:

  • Do not click on links you were not expecting
  • Be cautious of prizes, gifts, or special offers
  • Verify the sender before taking action
  • Never share passwords or sensitive information

One careless click can compromise personal data, systems, and even entire organizations. Staying alert and cautious is one of the most effective ways to protect yourself and those around you.
